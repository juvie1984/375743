Traveler tips

Small things that make Japan travel smoother, especially for students and first-time visitors.

👟

Wear comfortable shoes

Expect lots of walking at temples, stations, museums, shopping streets, and shrine grounds.

💴

Carry some cash

Japan is card-friendly in many places, but small shops, temples, and local food spots may still prefer cash.

🧃

Use convenience stores

Conbini are great for snacks, drinks, breakfast items, umbrellas, and quick travel essentials.

🙏

Respect shrine etiquette

Keep voices low, follow signs, avoid blocking paths, and ask before taking photos in sacred areas.

🚆

Stay station-ready

Keep tickets, IC cards, and small bags handy. Stations can be busy and fast-moving.

🌦️

Pack for June weather

June can be warm and humid with rain, so bring a light rain jacket or compact umbrella.
